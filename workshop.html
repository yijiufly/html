The 1st Riverside Security and Privacy Workshop (RSPW)

Introduction

UCR CRESP Center (UCR Center for Research and Education in Cyber Security and Privacy) plans to organize a workshop on security and privacy on an annual basis, to stimulate R&D and education in security and privacy in Southern California and beyond. More specifically, we would like to use this opportunity to:

  • Stimulate research collaborations among universities and colleges
  • Provide solutions to emerging and long-standing challenges faced by cybersecurity industry
  • Attract students to join security and privacy research

    • Format

    The workshop is expected to be a one-day event with the following setup

  • Keynote speech. We will invite one or two distinguished researchers to give keynote speech.
  • Presentations. We invite industry leaders, professors, and researchers to present their recent work.
  • Poster session.
  • Ad-hoc conversation during lunch and breaks.
